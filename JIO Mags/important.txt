First to configure you need a rooted phone or emulator like bluestacks.
Login to your jio news app.
With the use of any explorer which has root access [preferably Root Explorer] go to /data/data/com.jio.media.jioxpressnews/shared_prefs/jionews_preference.xml
Copy the following info within the brackets and paste it in respective txt files

Refer to jio.png
access_token
private_key
uuid
Run the application from "main_scripy.py" directly by opening the file. Do not use idle or other IDE
